About me

I'm Lecturer from Universitas Muhammadiyah Yogyakarta, Indonesia, focussing research in computer vision and artificial intelligence. I enjoy turning complex research problems into simple solutions that bridge the gap between theory and real-world applications in computer vision and AI.

Now, I'm studying in Tokyo Metropolitan University, Japan. My research topic is sport analysis using computer vision. My aim is to develop techniques that utilize AI to enhance the understanding and performance of athletes.
